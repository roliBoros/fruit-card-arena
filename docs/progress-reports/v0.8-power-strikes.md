# v0.8 — Power Strikes, Combos and Battle Juice

## Goal

Make combat moment-to-moment interactive and visually flashy, following current
mobile-game feedback patterns (timed inputs, combo escalation, screen shake,
particles, crit flashes).

## What changed

### Gameplay

- **Power Strike gauge:** Attack, Special and +20 Bonus now open a timing
  gauge. A marker ping-pongs across the bar; tap to lock it.
  - Gold centre zone → **PERFECT**: 1.5× damage critical with flash and shake.
  - Green zone → Good: normal damage.
  - Edges → Weak: 0.75× damage.
  - Auto-resolves as Good after ~4.6 s, and instantly for players with
    `prefers-reduced-motion` — nobody is locked out of the game.
- **Combo streak:** consecutive offensive turns that land damage build a combo
  (+8% damage per stack, capped at 5). Whiffed attacks break it; Guard keeps
  it. A gold COMBO ×N badge pops over the arena from ×2.
- Engine: `resolveTurn` gained an optional `playerPower` multiplier applied to
  the player's attack/bonus/special damage. Covered by two new tests (17 total).

### Presentation

- Impact particle bursts on every landed hit (colour-coded: red hit, gold
  crit, green heal, purple special).
- Arena screen shake — small for normal hits, big for criticals.
- Radial crit flash over the arena on PERFECT strikes.
- Damage numbers scale with the amount; crits show gold with a CRIT! tag.
- New synthesized crit and combo sounds with matching haptic patterns.

## Verification

- 17/17 Vitest tests pass; TypeScript build clean.
- Verified live in browser: gauge sweep, tap-to-lock, PERFECT crit path
  (flash + shake-big + burst + "CRITICAL!" message + 35 vs 24 base damage),
  combo badge ×2 → ×3, reduced-motion auto-resolve, zero console errors.
